# Future Workflow Concepts

## Infrastructure Assessment Workflow
Analyzes operational systems, dependencies, risks, bottlenecks, and resilience.

## Workforce Intelligence Workflow
Evaluates staffing, training, retention, and operational capability gaps.

## Strategic Planning Workflow
Coordinates long-term enterprise planning and systems analysis.

## Enterprise Risk Workflow
Identifies operational, financial, technical, and organizational risks.

## Policy Impact Workflow
Evaluates the operational effects of regulations and policy changes.

## Digital Twin Workflow
Creates operational models for enterprise simulation and forecasting.
